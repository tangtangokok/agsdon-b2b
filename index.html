Quality Guarantee

Built to Last.
Checked at Every Step.

Our ISO 9001-certified process includes 6 mandatory inspection gates — from raw material to finished carton.

✓
Raw Material Inspection
All incoming leather, fabric, and components tested for color fastness, tensile strength, and chemical compliance (REACH, CPSIA).
✓
In-Line QC (3 Gates)
Cutting accuracy, stitching quality, and lasting precision checked at every production stage by dedicated QC staff.
✓
Final Random Inspection (AQL 2.5)
Every shipment undergoes AQL 2.5 sampling before release. Third-party inspection available on request (SGS, BV, Intertek).
✓
Packaging & Loading Supervision
Carton drop tests, barcode scanning, and container loading supervision to ensure goods arrive intact.
